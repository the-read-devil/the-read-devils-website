Why Join?

How It Works

  1. Request an invite (limited seats; we keep cohorts small).
  2. Pick a reading pledge—pages per day or week.
  3. Join check‑ins; share one insight that stayed with you.
  4. Join city meetups in Bengaluru when you can.

FAQs

Who can join?

Anyone who is curious. If you’ve ever underlined a sentence because it made you pause, you’ll fit right in.

Is there a fee?

Membership is free. In‑person meetups are usually pay‑your‑own‑way. Occasionally, we’ll contribute for venues or screenings.

Online or in person?

Both. We have regular online check‑ins, and city chapters meet when schedules (and weather) allow.

Why invite‑only?

Small, consistent cohorts work better than large, inactive groups. Invite‑only helps maintain focus and tone.

Do I need to be a heavy reader?

No. If 20 pages feels ambitious, we’ll help you ease in. Progress over perfection.

Where are you based?

Bengaluru. Most of our in‑person magic happens here—coffee, museums, and book swaps. Online cohorts run in parallel.

Can I join if I’m not in Bengaluru?

Yes. You’ll join an online cohort for check‑ins and discussions. Visiting Bengaluru? Pull up a chair at a meetup.
