Why Join

  • Build a reading habit with gentle accountability.
  • Share “mind-blowing moments” from books, films, music, and art.
  • Real conversations, zero-judgement.
  • Small cohorts and real-world meetups when possible.
  • Curated prompts and theme months to keep you inspired.
  • Fun over pressure; progress over perfection.

What You’ll Do

  • 20-page/day commitment sprints.
  • “Mind-blowing moments” threads to swap insights.
  • Theme months (poetry, short stories, world cinema, etc.).
  • Lunches/outings, occasional book swaps, museum/film nights.

How It Works

  1. Request an invite (limited seats; we keep cohorts small).
  2. Pick a reading pledge—pages per day or week.
  3. Show up for check-ins; share one insight that moved you.
  4. Join city meetups when you can.

FAQs

Who can join?

Anyone curious about reading and conversations.

Is there a fee?

Currently free; some events may be pay-your-own-way.

Online or in person?

Both—online discussions plus optional city meetups.

Why invite-only?

To keep groups focused, respectful, and consistent.

Do I need to be a heavy reader?

No—beginners welcome.

Where are you based?

Mostly India; online is open.
